CH.03 — The Internship

4 months in
the delivery loop

At Artery Partner I built Google Workspace integrations for Frappe/ERPNext, configured GCP IAM service accounts, and automated internal workflows with a scheduled GitLab CI/CD pipeline — then wired up Grafana dashboards and explored Prometheus instrumentation to make that work observable.

I learned that the hard part isn't writing the YAML — it's making deploys boring: predictable, observable, and safe to roll back. That mindset now shapes everything I build.
